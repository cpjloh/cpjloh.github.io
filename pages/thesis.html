Sensor Emulation and ML for Collaborative AV in Simuations (M.S. Thesis)

September 2019 - December 2020

My master's thesis will be contributions towards an industry-sponsored vehicle simulator used to study collaborative autonomous vehicles and their potential security vulnerabilities.

At the time of this writing, it seems as though my specific contributions will be sensor emulation and developing machine learning algorithms to train collaborative vehicles (i.e. how to navigate an intersection without the use of traffic signals).

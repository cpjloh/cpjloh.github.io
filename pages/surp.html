Summer Undergraduate Researcher

June 2018 - August 2018

Under the guidance of Charles Birdsong PhD, Professor of Mechanical Engineering at Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo, I worked on the implementation of lane tracking using Simulink and MATLAB on a small-scale intelligent vehicle. My work was conducted in a research environment, which means I conducted a literature review, provided oral and written updates to Professor Birdsong, and wrote a final report on my work. We presented our work at the Mechanical Engineering Industrial Advisory Board (IAB) and at the Summer Undergraduate Research Program (SURP) Poster Symposium.

The final report and poster are available upon request.
